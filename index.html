About Me

I'm a self-taught frontend developer based in Spain. I started programming as a hobby, thanks to a friend who showed me what was possible to do with a bit of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I decided to start to learn with some platforms like FreeCodeCamp and The Odin Project. Once I felt comfortable with the basics, I joined the Codeacademy platform, I bought some courses in Udemy and the book: "Eloquent JavaScript: A Modern Introduction to Programming", meanwhile I was clearing doubts using Google and Youtube.

At some point, I realized that I was feeling really excited every time I could solve a new algorithm or implement a new skill. That was when I decided to become a professional developer.

But this means that, once you start learning you can't stop. that's why continuous learning is a core part of my approach to career development. In order to keep on learning, I employ personal qualities of: self motivation, observation and curiosity.
